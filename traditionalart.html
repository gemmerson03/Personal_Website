Traditional Art

Line Project

Two cats are framed using the rule of thirds. Directional, vertical and implied lines are explored. Various shading techniques are applied, such as hatching, cross-hatching, scumbling and stippling.

Value Project

This project depicts value, space, and time using a snow sculpture of a gator. The range of values consists of high-key and low-key areas. Light and shadow contrast to create an illusion of volume and space.
